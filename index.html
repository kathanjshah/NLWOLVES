WOLVES NL Cricket Club is a new incorporated team established in 2021 and will debut in the upcoming summer league.
Our primary goal is to contribute towards the future of cricket in Newfoundland by coming up with a team of youngsters
and raise the bars of the competition in the summer league, giving opportunity to young players and nurture them to excel in the upcoming tournaments. WOLVES NL is more than just a team, ‘PACK OF WOLVES’ might be an absolute phrase we may use to define the relationship
among our team members. We are looking forward to be a part of healthy and competitive League this summer.
